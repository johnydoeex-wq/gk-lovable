Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 11, 2026

GK Lovable ("Extension", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected when you use the GK Lovable Chrome Extension.

1. Information We Collect

The extension may collect or store the following information only as required for its functionality:

  • User preferences and extension settings
  • Locally stored configuration data
  • Authentication/session information (when required)
  • Website information necessary to provide extension features

We do not sell your personal information.

2. How Information Is Used

The collected information is used only to:

  • Provide extension functionality
  • Improve user experience
  • Maintain login sessions (when applicable)
  • Store user preferences
  • Enable AI productivity features

3. Chrome Permissions Used

Permission Purpose
activeTab Access the active Lovable webpage after user interaction.
tabs Detect supported browser tabs and enable extension features.
storage Save extension settings and preferences locally.
sidePanel Display the AI assistant within Chrome's Side Panel.
scripting Inject extension scripts into supported webpages after user interaction.
cookies Maintain authentication sessions only when necessary.
Host Permissions Interact only with supported Lovable websites to provide extension functionality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties except when required to provide the extension's functionality or to comply with applicable laws.

5. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect stored information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the extension's functionality or until the user removes the extension or clears stored data.

7. Third-Party Services

GK Lovable may communicate with supported websites or APIs required to provide its features. Those services are governed by their own privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

The extension is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
